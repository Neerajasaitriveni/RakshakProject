   Emergency Guide

Here are the few steps inorder to escape from few types of threatening situations

Fire accident

Yell "Fire!" several times and go outside right away. If you live in a building with elevators, use the stairs. Leave all your things where they are and save yourself. If closed doors or handles are warm or smoke blocks your primary escape route, use your second way out.

Road Condition

Our website is designed to address various road conditions,Whether it maybe climatic changes,unexpected obstacles,Potholes,reduced visibility, slippery roads.It gives the location information to the respected emergency services

Mechanical Failure

Encountering a mechanical failure is difficult, but our website is here to guide users through the situation,Users are advised not to attempt repairs unless trained, and instead, to call for professional assistance through our platform, using Hazarad lights would help user to overcome the situation.

Reckless Driving

Our platform addresses the risks associated with fast driving by encouraging responsible behavior on the road, Users can report incidents to law enforcement through the website, contributing to safer roads

Women Safety and Protection

With a dedicated focus on women's safety, our system incorporates features to address potential threats or emergencies. In situations where personal safety is compromised, users can trigger distress signals through the system, instantly alerting local authorities and predefined contacts.

Failure to Obey Traffic Rules

Our comprehensive monitoring system actively scans for violations of traffic rules, including instances like drunk driving, crossing red signal lights, triple riding, and using mobile phones while driving. When a violation is detected, the system promptly issues immediate alerts to both the driver and pertinent law enforcement authorities.
